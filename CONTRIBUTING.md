# Contributing

**Use, run, fork, and build on** this project however you like. That is what the Apache-2.0 license is for.

**This repository does not accept contributions.** It is a small companion project, kept as a stable,
single-maintainer reference in the same spirit as the upstream it complements. To keep it simple and
predictable:

- **Pull requests are not accepted.** Please do not open one; it will be closed unmerged. Fork the repo
  and make the change on your own copy instead.
- **The issue tracker is turned off** for the same reason.
- Found something broken, or have an idea? You are welcome to fork and change it freely. You can also
  reach the author through their GitHub profile at https://github.com/w4ester, with no promise of a
  response or a fix.

None of this limits what you can do with the code. Under Apache-2.0 you may copy it, modify it, and
ship your own version. This policy only means the *original* repo stays a fixed point.
